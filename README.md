# 🌊 RIVER | Real-Time Market Intelligence Terminal

**RIVER** is an enterprise-grade data analytics platform that provides a transparent "window" into the micro-economy of Nairobi's wards. By aggregating real-time transaction data from the **Sauti Pesa** merchant network, RIVER transforms raw trade activity into actionable insights for institutional data buyers.

---

## 📊 Key Analytical Capabilities

### 1. Geographic Market Intelligence
* **Ward-Level Analysis**: Filter economic activity by specific wards (e.g., Mowlem, Eastleigh, Umoja) to identify hyper-local demand.
* **Aggregated Metrics**: Track Total Sales, Total Debt, and Inventory Volume per geographic region.

### 2. Consumer Behavior & Market Share
* **Product Performance**: Real-time market share tracking for individual product categories (Sugar, Soap, Bread, etc.).
* **Buying Patterns**: Analysis of time-of-day demand spikes and "bundle" purchasing trends (e.g., products frequently bought together).

### 3. Credit & Risk Assessment
* **Debt-to-Cash Ratios**: Visualizing the relationship between cash sales and recorded credit (Kopa) to determine ward-level financial health.
* **Merchant Velocity**: Tracking how quickly merchants turn over inventory and settle digital records.

---

## 📥 Data Terminal & Export
RIVER provides a raw data feed for deep institutional analysis. Institutional partners can export live datasets in **CSV format** across the following categories:
* **Cash & M-Pesa Sales**: High-fidelity transaction logs.
* **Inventory & Stock**: Real-time supply levels across the network.
* **Debts & Expenses**: Granular credit and operational cost tracking.
* **CRM & Appointments**: Service-sector engagement metrics.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL) for real-time data streaming.
* **Frontend**: GitHub Pages (Static Hosting) with Tailwind CSS.
* **Visuals**: Chart.js for dynamic market share and trend visualization.
* **Logic**: Vanilla JavaScript utilizing the Supabase SDK.
